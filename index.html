Our Mission


Project Bayanihan supports schools and underserved communities in Sudipen and beyond by providing supplies, volunteer-led tutoring, and community-driven programs. We believe small, consistent acts of service add up.

Get Involved

We run almost entirely on volunteer energy. Here are simple ways you can help:

  • Volunteer as an online tutor (1 hour/week)
  • Help run our social media & fundraising posts
  • Translate resources between English and Ilocano/Tagalog
  • Donate School Supplies
